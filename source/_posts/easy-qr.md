# EasyQR Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how EasyQR ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use the EasyQR mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

EasyQR is designed to work offline. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data generated by the App remains on your device.

The App maintains a local history of QR codes you have scanned or created. This history is stored exclusively on your device and is never shared with us or any third party. You can clear this history at any time from within the App.

---

## 2. Permissions We Request

### Camera
We request camera access solely to enable QR code scanning. The camera feed is processed locally on your device in real time and is never recorded or transmitted.

### Photos / Media
We request photo library access to allow you to:
- Select images from your gallery for QR code scanning
- Add a custom logo to generated QR codes
- Save generated QR codes to your gallery

We do not read, copy, or transmit any other photos.

### Contacts
We request contacts access solely to let you import contact information when creating a Contact QR code. Contact data is used only within the App to populate input fields and is not stored or uploaded anywhere.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services provided by Google, which may collect information to identify you or improve their services. Please review their respective privacy policies for more information:

- [Google Play Services](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Google ML Kit](https://developers.google.com/ml-kit/terms)

---

## 4. Log Data

In the event of an error in the App, we may collect diagnostic data through third-party tools (such as crash reporting). This may include information such as your device model, operating system version, and the time and date of the error. This information is used solely for debugging and improving the App.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

This App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take immediate action to remove that information.

---

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App. Changes are effective immediately upon posting. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any updates.

---

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the **Feedback** option in the App settings.
